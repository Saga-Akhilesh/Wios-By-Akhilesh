Data Analytics

The science of analyzing raw data to make conclusions about that information is Data analytics. Exploring large datasets to find hidden patterns, unseen trends, discovering correlations, and deriving valuable insights to make predictions are some concepts we delve into in this realm.Many of the techniques and processes of data analytics have been automated into mechanical processes and algorithms that work over raw data for human consumption.

Objective

  • To establish clearly the objectives and scope of the predictive analysis.
  • Using R programming language to identify suitable data sources to agree the methodological approach.
  • Validate and review data accurately and identify anomalies.
  • To appreciate the current trends in data analysis procedure.
  • Carry out rule-based analysis of the data in line with the analysis plan.

    • Outcomes

  • Understanding R with Business Intelligence, Business Analytics, Data and Information
  • Learning how to contextually integrate and correlate information automatically to gain faster insights
  • Implementing statistical analysis techniques for solving practical problems.
  • Graphically interpreting data and Finding meaningful patterns.
  • Performing statistical analysis on a variety of data.
